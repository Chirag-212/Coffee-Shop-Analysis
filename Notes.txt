Objective: The main objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to gain actionable insights that will enhance the performance of coffee shop.

Question to answer:
1. How do sales vary by the day of the week and hour of the day.
2. Are there any peak time for sales activity
3. What is total sales revenue for each month?
4. How do sales vary across different store locations?
5. What is average price/order per person
6. What products are best selling in terms of quantity and revenue
7. How do Sales vary by category of different products

Data Source: Coffee Shop Sales: https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground
